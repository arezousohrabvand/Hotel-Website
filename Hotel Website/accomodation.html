Accomodation

Deluxe King Suite

The grandest of our suites features nearly 1000 square feet of elegant accommodations and has a spectacular view of the lush Humber Valley Parklands. The gracious and sophisticated décor of this suite features hardwood floors, hand-woven rugs, separate dining and living areas, wet bar, and an all-season fireplace. The spacious bathroom features an oversized deep-soaking Jacuzzi tub for two and a separate glass shower.

Luxury King Suite

Our Luxury King Suite is a one-of-a-kind suite with a king-sized four-poster Bombay bed, a spacious bedroom, and living room, plus magnificent private dining rooms with a stunning view. The spacious bathroom provides a fabulous Jacuzzi tub for two with a separate glass shower.

Deluxe Queen Courtyard Suite

Our Deluxe Courtyard Suite is one of two exclusive suites located on the top floor. Take in the stunning panoramic view of the Humber Valley and the award-winning Old Mill gardens from the over-sized arched windows. This suite features a cozy bedroom with a luxurious queen-sized bed and a stunningly appointed, bright and spacious bathroom with a Jacuzzi tub for two.

Premium King Suite

Our Premium King Suite features a magnificent Tudor style luxury with an inviting king bed. Unwind by the all season fireplace in the comfortably elegant living room with hardwood floors and a hand-woven rug. Relax further in a stunning bathroom with a fabulous Jacuzzi tub for two. (Décor varies)
