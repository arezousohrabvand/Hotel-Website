Afternoon Tea

A grand tradition for over a century, the Old Mill Toronto has the pleasure of serving you England’s most beloved ritual of afternoon tea. Unwind, and indulge with our house-made signature scones, scrumptious artisan tea sandwiches, and dainty French pastries. A unique tea experience served in our beautiful Terrace room overlooking the picturesque courtyard. To compliment such a special menu, we have teamed up with a local favourite, Sloane Fine Tea Merchants and hand picked 12 new signature Sloane teas that best represent our English roots. Whether it’s an afternoon out or a special occasion with a group of friends, our Afternoon Tea experience is a perfect way to savour your special moments and enjoy in conversation over an assortment of treats. We invite you to Old Mill Toronto to relax with a hot cup of tea and enjoy your afternoon! Old Mill Toronto donates a portion of the proceeds from the Afternoon Tea annually to Women’s Habitat, our charity of choice.
