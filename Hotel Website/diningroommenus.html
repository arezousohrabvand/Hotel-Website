Dinnig Menus

Enjoy an elegant fine dining experience in the historical setting of Old Mill Toronto Dining Room.
We are delighted to offer a Special Seasonal menu for groups of 20 or more. We ask that you choose one dessert for your entire group prior to your group’s arrival. A final guarantee of the number of guests attending should be made with the restaurant Supervisor three (3) business days prior to your function. Where no guarantee of attendance is received, your function will be prepared and charged for according to the original estimated figure.
Professional Audio Visual Productions are disturbing to fellow guests and as such are not permitted. (Limited Private/Personal videoing is permitted) Prices are subject to change without notice. Reservations required 416-207-2020 or Book online
Monday & Tuesday – Closed Wednesday to Saturday from 6:00 pm – 8:30 pm Taxes: Are not included in pricing.
Gratuity Policy: 15% gratuity will apply to groups of 10 or more.
Settlement Policy: Only one bill will be provided for the entire group/party. Multiple credit card or other payment settlements will be accepted.
Reservation Policy: For groups of 6 or more, a $10 per person credit card deposit is required. (The deposit will be applied against the final bill).
Cancellation Policy:In the event that a group reservation is cancelled on the date, the reservation is being held for, the full deposit will be non-refundable.
