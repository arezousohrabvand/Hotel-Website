Join us for our Festive holiday Lunch and Dinner Buffets!

Festive Celebrations at Old Mill Toronto have been a tradition enjoyed for over a century! Our glorious Christmas decorations and wood-burning fireplaces radiate a warm and festive atmosphere second to none! Executive Chef Martin Buehner and his talented culinary team have once again crafted a splendid Festive Lunch & Dinner Buffet in the Old Mill Dining Room. With so many festive season favourites to choose from, you will have to make some difficult dining decisions! Bring your family, friends and colleagues, and get into the holiday spirit!
