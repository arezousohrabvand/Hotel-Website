Welcome to Old Mill Toronto

Nestled in the beautiful Humber Valley and adjacent to the Parkland and Toronto Bike & Walking Trail System, our ideal location invites you to Escape the Ordinary. Whether you are seeking a romantic weekend getaway, luxury spa visit, meeting or event space, beautiful wedding venue or delicious dining, elegant hospitality is consistently redefined at Old Mill Toronto.
