Nutcracker Afternoon Tea

Nutcracker Afternoon Tea at Old Mill Toronto

The nutcracker sits under the holiday tree, a guardian of childhood stories. Feed him walnuts and he will crack open a tale…

Our Nutcracker Tea is our seasonal gift to you! Your favourite holiday flavours are making a debut Over a century of tea service precision Our afternoon tea is always the right decision Savour it now, and until the festive season bids us adieu May afternoon tea at the Old Mill Toronto always bring holiday joy to you! A grand tradition for over a century, the Old Mill Toronto has the pleasure of serving you England’s most beloved ritual of afternoon tea. Unwind, and indulge with our house-made signature scones, scrumptious artisan tea sandwiches, and dainty French pastries. Old Mill Toronto donates a portion of the proceeds from the Afternoon Tea annually to Women’s Habitat, our charity of choice.
